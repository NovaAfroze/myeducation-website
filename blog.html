Our Certificate & activities for 2024

Pubergaon Polytechnic and Medical Institute (PPMI) is one of the best and biggest private polytechnic and Medical Institute in Bangladesh. It was established in 2011 with only 3(three) departments of diploma -in- engineering in Electrical, Civil, and Computer technology with growing demands of mid-level technical manpower at home and abroad the institute has since greatly expanded.


Pubergaon Polytechnic Institute is the official name of the academic institute, as registered with the education authorities. The native or alternative name of the institute is পাবেরগাঁও পলিটেকনিক ইনস্টিটিউট, often used in local Bengali language contexts. The unique Educational Institute Identification Number (EIIN) assigned to this institute is 136121, provided by the Directorate of Secondary and Higher Education.
