Diploma In Textile

The largest employment sector in Bangladesh is the textile industry. Every year, thousands of skilled textile engineers are needed in this sector. However, the supply of skilled textile diploma engineers falls short of the demand. Currently, the country has 445 spinning factories, 796 fabric manufacturing factories, 240 dyeing, printing, and finishing factories, and over 5,500 garment factories. The primary goal of this course is to develop skilled textile engineers to sustain the country's largest export-oriented industrial sector.
